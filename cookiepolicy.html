What are cookies?

Cookies are small portions of data contained within text files and they are saved to devices such as your computer when websites are loaded inside a browser. Session cookies are temporary cookies that are used while you're using the website, they are removed upon closing the browser. Persistent cookies are cookies that remain on your device after you close the browser or restart your device, they are used to store your preferences and other such data within this website.
